Product details

Upgrade your kitchen with this value combo: the Hisense Manual Microwave Oven paired with the Boscon 2.0L Stainless Steel Electric Kettle.

The Hisense microwave features simple mechanical dial controls for power level and timer, a spacious 20-litre cavity and a defrost setting — perfect for quickly heating, defrosting and cooking everyday meals.

The Boscon BOS-EK20S electric kettle boils water fast thanks to its concealed heating element, generous 2.0-litre capacity and durable brushed stainless steel body. It includes auto shut-off and boil-dry protection for safe, effortless use.

Together they make the ideal starter set for any home, office, dorm or kitchen — stylish, reliable and ready to use straight out of the box.
